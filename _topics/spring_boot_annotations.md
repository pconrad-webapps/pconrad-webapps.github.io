Spring and Spring Boot rely on many annotations.

Here are just a few:

```
@Autowired
@Bean
@Component
@ComponentScan
@Configuration
@Controller
@EnableAutoConfiguration
@Repository
@RestController
@Service
@SpringBootApplication
@SpringBootConfiguration
```
